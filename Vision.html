Vision



Vision



To recalibrate how we view potential, success and happiness so that the next generation is happier and more fulfilled.


Every day, we are faced with a new generation that is born. These young minds bring fresh perspectives to the world in which they live and provide us all an opportunity for innovation if given the right encouragement. We want to help parents and teachers to give them this encouragement.


Korroboree is a sacred gathering of learners. We exist for all those in the learning ecosystem. Parent, teacher, child. We are an online learning platform that helps parents and educators guide and connect with children so that children discover the power of play, creativity, and curiosity.


Parents and teachers will have the information, tools, resources and learning opportunities so they can mindfully invest in their children and learners. This will build the next generation to have more empowered hearts, minds, bodies and souls. In this process parents and teachers can help create a world that is more loving, joyous, peaceful and abundant.


Parents and Teachers will also be able to guide young learners to select tools and resources for self-paced, personalised learning so children can explore their passion while developing skills like grit, empathy, emotional regulation, self-awareness. We believe that every child has something special inside of them waiting to be discovered – we just need the right tools! That’s why we created Korroboree - so parents can find those tools for their children and nurture their natural talents from an early age.


Korroboree is a human development platform that engages parents towards mindful parenting and teachers towards mindful learning so that children become the best version of themselves.


Korroboree is an online learning platform for children and parents to learn together. We want to move the parent mindset away from an outer-in version of success to an inner-out version of success and transition towards self-driven learning based on a child's passion and purpose.
